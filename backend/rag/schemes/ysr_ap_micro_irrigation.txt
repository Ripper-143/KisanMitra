Andhra Pradesh Micro Irrigation Project (APMIP)
State Subsidy Scheme.
1. Eligibility: Farmers holding cultivable land in Andhra Pradesh. Targeted primarily at small and marginal farmers growing horticulture crops, cotton, or sugarcane.
2. Benefits: Offers up to 90% subsidy for small and marginal farmers (under 5 acres) and 70% subsidy for other farmers for drip irrigation and sprinkler systems. Helps conserve water, reduces labor requirements, and boosts crop quality.
3. How to Apply: Apply online through the APMIP portal or visit the local Rythu Bharosa Kendra (RBK) with land documents (Pattadar passbook), Aadhaar card, and bank account details.
