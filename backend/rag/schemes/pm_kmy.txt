Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)
Central Sector Government Pension Scheme.
1. Eligibility: All small and marginal farmers in India aged between 18 and 40 years. Landholding must be equal to or less than 2 hectares (5 acres).
2. Benefits: Assured monthly pension of Rs. 3,000 to eligible farmers after attaining the age of 60 years.
3. Contributions: Voluntary contribution from Rs. 55 to Rs. 200 per month depending on age. The Central Government matches the contribution.
4. How to Apply: Apply online via the PM-KMY portal or visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC). Carry Aadhaar Card and Savings Bank Account details.
